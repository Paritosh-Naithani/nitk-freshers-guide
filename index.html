About the Game

NITK Fresher's Guide — Visual Novel

NITK Fresher's Guide is an interactive visual novel crafted to ease incoming students into the world of NIT Karnataka, Surathkal — one of India's most beautiful (and occasionally chaotic) campuses on the Arabian Sea coast.

Step into the shoes of a first-year student arriving on campus for the very first time. Meet seniors who'll become mentors and friends, navigate the maze of academic blocks, and discover the unwritten rules of hostel life — all through branching dialogue and real choices that matter.

🏛 Campus Exploration 🤝 Social Choices 📚 Academic Survival 🎭 Clubs & Events 🌊 NITK Culture 🏠 Hostel Life 🎮 Ren'Py Engine

No downloads, no installs — plays entirely in your browser. Perfect for reading the night before your first day, or sharing with your future batchmates.
